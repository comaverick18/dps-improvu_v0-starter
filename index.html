How It Works

PRACTICE

Have AI practice conversations with you

Practice conversations in a low-stakes environment with an AI partner that dynamically responds to your practice sessions and teaches you improvisational skills through real-time dialogue.

1 Choose a conversation scenario (job interview, first date, networking)
2 Chat with AI that introduces unexpected conversational turns
3 Receive instant feedback on "yes-and" and active listening
CHALLENGE

Complete daily confidence quests

Gamify building confidence through small, daily conversational missions. Real application, low stakes.

1 Receive daily quests like "Ask a barista a non-coffee question"
2 Mark quests complete and journal about the experience
3 Level up, unlock achievements, and grow your confidence score
CONNECT

Practice with a supportive community

Connect with other users to practice improvisational skills and challenging conversations one-on-one or in group sessions. Build a network of friends and mentors.

1 Unlock after completing successful quests
2 Select your level and join appropriate groups
3 Attend sessions guided by improv experts
